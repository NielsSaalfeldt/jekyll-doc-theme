What is our project?

Our project consisted in creating a data set of the available jobs in Switzerland and to be able to map them. We scrapped from the website Indeed.ch and used the leaflet package. We came up with this idea because we are currently looking for an internship.Therefore we decided to use the opportunity of this course to build an app that would enable and help us to reach our ultimate goal.

Our app allows the user to filter jobs category according to the domain he wishes to engage itself. The categories are the following : Marketing, HR, Data Science, Consulting, Accounting/Finance, Business, Management, Economics, Communication. We chose to focus on these categories as they are inline with our studies, other categories seemed not relevant scrapping and time consuming.
